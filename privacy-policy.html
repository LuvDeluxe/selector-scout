Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 12, 2025

Introduction

Selector Scout is a Chrome extension designed to assist web developers and QA automation engineers in generating CSS selectors and detecting accessibility issues on the active webpage. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your information.

Information We Collect

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data. It operates entirely locally within your browser:

  • When you activate the extension, it temporarily accesses the DOM (Document Object Model) of the current webpage via the "activeTab" permission to analyze elements for selector generation and accessibility checks.
  • No data is saved beyond the current session, and nothing is sent to external servers.
  • If the extension uses local storage (e.g., for user preferences like framework selection), this is limited to non-sensitive, anonymized settings stored only on your device.

How We Use Information

Any temporary access to webpage content is solely to provide the extension's core functionality. We do not use, share, or process this information for any other purpose, including analytics, advertising, or personalization.

Sharing Information

We do not share any user data with third parties. All processing happens locally on your device.

Data Retention

No data is retained after you close the extension or browser session.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if the extension's functionality changes. We'll notify users via the Chrome Web Store.
